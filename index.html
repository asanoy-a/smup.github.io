About 14 objects in SpectraM14

This dataset includes 14 objects, each exhibiting a range of optical properties such as monochromatic or multicolored appearances, and diffuse or specular reflections.

Main dataset

This dataset consists of images captured under three different lighting conditions.

  • Six colors of light were each independently illuminated.
  • Three colors of light were simultaneously illuminated.
  • Five NIR lights were each independently illuminated.
